GoodsCart!

GoodsCart Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Maharashtra, India. Founded by Sayali Sonavane in 2020 , the company initially focused onconsumer electronics,, before expanding into other product categories such as fashion, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.[5][6] as of March 2017, GoodsCart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.[7] GoodsCart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel (a position that was bolstered by its acquisitions of Myntra and Jabong.com), and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.
